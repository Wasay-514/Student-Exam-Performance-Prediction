
Student Performance Dataset - Data Dictionary

StudentID: Unique student identifier
Gender: Male/Female
Age: Student age
StudyHoursPerDay: Daily study hours
AttendancePercentage: Attendance percentage
AssignmentsCompleted: Number of assignments completed
SleepHours: Daily sleep hours
PreviousGrade: Previous exam percentage
InternetUsageHours: Daily internet usage
ExtraClasses: Takes extra classes or not
Result: Target variable (Pass/Fail)
